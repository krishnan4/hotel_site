Our Story

Cooking with Love & Tradition

We opened our doors with a single griddle and a family recipe that had been passed down for three generations. Today, that same batter — fermented overnight, seasoned with love — is the foundation of everything we serve.

From the evening rush of crispy dosas to late-night plates of kothu parotta, we are here when hunger calls. No shortcuts, no compromises. Just real South Indian cooking, done right.

What We Serve

The Full Menu

Onion Dosa
Crispy rice crepe loaded with caramelised onions, curry leaves, and green chilli.
Onion Uttapam
Thick, spongy rice pancake topped with onions, tomatoes, and coriander.
Egg Dosa
Golden dosa folded over a spiced egg filling — an all-time favourite.
Podi Dosa
Dosa rubbed with gunpowder spice mix and finished with sesame oil. Fiery and bold.
Idli
Soft, fluffy steamed rice cakes served with sambar and traditional chutneys.
Kal Dosa
Soft, spongy, slightly thick crepes served hot as a pair, perfect with salna.
Plain Dosa
Classic crispy, golden-brown rolled rice crepe, served with sambar and chutneys.
Kothu Parotta
Shredded flaky parotta tossed on the griddle with egg, onion, and masala. A classic street dish.
Veechu Parotta
Paper-thin, layered flatbread flung and flipped by hand. Tender, flaky perfection.
Egg Veechu Parotta
Veechu parotta served with a fresh egg fry and coconut chutney.
Chapati
Soft, whole-wheat flatbreads toasted on the tawa, served warm with salna or kurma.
Omelette
Fluffy, spiced egg omelette with onion, tomato, and green chilli.
Half Boil Egg
Perfectly soft-boiled egg with a runny yolk — simple, satisfying.
Chicken Gravy
Rich, slow-cooked chicken in a tangy tomato-onion masala. Perfect with parotta.
Fish Fry
Crispy, spicy pan-fried fish steaks marinated in traditional South Indian spices.
Egg Podi Mass
South Indian style scrambled eggs tossed with onions, green chilies, and black pepper.
Egg Kalaki
A soft, folded, runny egg pocket seasoned with rich and spicy chicken salna.
